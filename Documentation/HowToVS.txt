The following is based loosly off of the instructions found at:
	https://github.com/mono/MonoGame/wiki/Tutorials:-Getting-Started-on-Windows-with-Visual-Studio

 1) Gain access to repository.  (GitHub.com)
 2) Get repository URL.
 3) Open VS 2013+.
 4) Open Team Explorer.
 5) Click "Connect to Team Projects".
 6) click "Clone".
	a) HTTPS URL to Repository.				("https://github.com/henryschade/PS-CFW.git")
	b) Local path to new Project/Developement space.	("C:\Projects\PS-CFW")
 7) Click Clone button.
 8) Home button in Team Explorer.
 9) Click "Unsynced Commits".
10) Click "Sync" button.
11) Should now have all the latest code.
